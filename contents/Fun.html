creepy, overly-attached doll

This doll sat on a shoulder strap and whacked anybody who came too close. My creepy little Halloween project, the overly-attached, curly-haired, cyborg-girlfriend (a so-called curlfriend), was made using an Arduino Uno, a bunch of micro-servos, a sonic range sensor, two red LED's, some plaster for making the face and lots of cardboard.
